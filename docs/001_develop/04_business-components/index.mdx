Genesis Platform Business Components are designed to give you working business solutions for the most common requirements of financial applications.

You can incorporate them into your application in very little time. They are easy to install, run and customise to meet your exact requirements.

Each one comes with a combination of pre-built microservices, APIs and UI components that can be integrated in to your application with minimal configuration.
