### What industries is the Genesis application platform meant for?

Although Genesis is versatile, it is specifically designed to address the needs of the financial markets. 

Its architecture and features make it adaptable to the unique challenges of financial services, providing solutions that are secure, real-time, and scalable.  

### What use cases is the Genesis Application Platform typically used for? 

The platform is highly flexible and supports a diverse use cases, including: 

- EUC/UDT replacements
- Trading and execution platforms for financial institutions 
- Real-time data processing and aggregation 
- Post-trade transaction systems 
- Critical market infrastructure
- Scalable regulatory and compliance solutions
